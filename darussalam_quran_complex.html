Darussalam Quran Complex

Darussalam Quran Complex is a very useful project of Darussalam International, giving far reaching results. The Quran Complex, covering a vast area of 5 acres, will be one of the most modern printing presses in the world. For the best printing of the Holy Quran, this complex will have no parallel. The construction of the Quran Complex is on its way and the machinery is to be installed soon. The mass publication of the Holy Quran and distribution of its translations and commentaries in various languages is one of its primary objectives for spreading the word of Allah.

Under the auspices of Darussalam Trust, in 2003 AD, a vast network of Quranic education for the religious training of boys and girls was established. Its branches have spread to distant rural and urban areas of Sheikhupura, Nankana, Gujranwala, Hafizabad, Lahore and Muzafargarh districts and they are patronized financially and educationally by honourable Abdul Malik Mujahid and his humane wife. رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنْهُمْ مَسَاعِيهِمْ. آمين!

By the Grace of Allah, this beautiful chain of Quranic education has expanded to approximately 200 religious schools and centers for boys and girls, in which number of students is more than 5000 who get Quranic teachings and contemporary education. فلله الحمد على ذلك. In these Quranic Centers, Darussalam Trust is financially sponsoring the needy and orphaned boys and girls and also their teachers and thus provides them opportunities to lead an honourable life.
