Food Package
for the Needy and the Homeless

By the Grace of Allah for last many years food packets are being distributed in the holy month of Ramadan among the workers of Darussalam, teachers of Quranic centers, poor families, relatives and friends. This food package consists of everyday food items. In 2013, the packets distributed weighed about 18 kgs.

Quranic Education
for Boys & Girls

Under the auspices of Darussalam Trust, in 2003, a vast network of Quranic education for the religious training of boys and girls was established. Its branches have spread to distant rural and urban areas of Sheikhupura, Nankana Sahib, Gujranwala, Hafizabad, Lahore and Muzzafargarh districts. They are patronized financially and educationally by Honourable Abdul Malik Mujahid and his wife.
