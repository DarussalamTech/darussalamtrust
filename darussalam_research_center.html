Darussalam Research Center

Darussalam Research Center is also a unique
project of Darussalam International.

The construction of this Research Center, lying in the middle of Lahore at Lower mall, is in progress rapidly. Its importance and utility can be judged from the fact that its two portions will be reserved for mosque only, whereas one floor for a modern library and one for auditorium, while one floor will be reserved for a unique project to display beautiful models of the Prophet’s era.

There is no doubt that Religious Madaris are those strongholds of the Muslim Ummah who provided defense to it against the movements of infidelity and atheism. These Madaris have been a great source of cultivating spiritual minds and many great figures and personalities emerged from these Madaris and worked hard for the cause of the true religion and against all the temptations in every field. After graduating from these Madaris our forefathers’ personalities reflected comprehensive and cognitive development. They were prominent in various fields of Arts and knowledge and held the highest place. They were at the same time scholars, teachers, judges, mufties, orators and have the efficiency and capacity to work on administrative positions.

At present we will have to admit that the performance of our religious institutions is not the same. Most of the graduates have been unable to offer a solution to contemporary problems. They have lagged behind due to the unawareness about today’s political, economical, social and civic problems. They have left vacant the field of action for the people having non-Islamic thoughts and this is a very alarming situation.

In practical, the number of Madaris is increasing day by day, the number of books available is increasing, sources are in abundance, improved transport facilities and other blessings which are available today were not available before but in spite of all these facilities there is a lack of people like our forefathers. What is the reason behind? According to educationists, there are many reasons of it. One of the reasons is lack of curriculum development according to the requirements, the ancient method of teaching, social problems and lack of awareness. It is important to reform the education system in Madaris and establishment of an exemplary training institution and “Professional Training College” for the training of the graduates of Madrasas which will provide them practical and professional training according to their abilities and the Ummah so that they can perform the task of guiding the Ummah in the best manner like our ancestors.
